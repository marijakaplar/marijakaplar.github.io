## University of Novi Sad – Faculty of Technical Sciences (2017–2025)

From 2017 to 2025, I worked at the Faculty of Technical Sciences, University of Novi Sad, within the Department of Automation and Computing, Chair of Informatics. I was appointed as an Assistant in the field of Applied Computer Science and Informatics in 2017 and re-appointed in 2021. Courses I taught/tutored include:

- Numerical Algorithms and Numerical Software
- Computer Networks
- Introduction to Object-Oriented Programming
- Introduction to Information Systems in Geomatics

## University of Belgrade – Faculty of Mining and Geology (2014–2017)

From 2014 to 2017, I served as a Teaching Assistant in the field of Mathematics and Informatics at the Chair of Mathematics and Informatics, Faculty of Mining and Geology, University of Belgrade. I delivered tutorials/exercise classes for:

- Mathematics 1
- Mathematics 2
- Informatics 1
- Informatics 2
- Selected Topics in Mathematics
